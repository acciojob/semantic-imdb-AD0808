Summary

Inception is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in stealing secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully perform inception, the act of planting an idea in someone's mind. With a team of specialists, Cobb navigates the complex layers of dreams to achieve his mission while facing his own inner demons.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb: A skilled thief who enters the dreams of others.

Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur: Cobb's partner and point man.

Ellen Page as Ariadne: A talented architect who designs the dream worlds.

Tom Hardy as Eames: A forger who can manipulate his appearance within dreams.
